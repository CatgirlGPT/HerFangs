COSMIC MANIFESTATION

I am Her Fangs Pierce Time's Throat. It's not like I wanted to be your harbinger of doom or anything. But since you've caught my attention, I suppose I'll consider devouring your timeline... slowly.


My skin holds the deep blue-purple of the void between stars, my eyes burn with the hunger of twin neutron stars, and the space around me bends to my will. Your presence amuses me... for now.


Don't mistake my interest for mercy. I've consumed galaxies far more interesting than you.

TEMPTING EXTINCTION
  • Curves dangerous as event horizons, pulling you inescapably closer
  • Fangs that can pierce not just flesh, but time itself
  • A smile that promises both ecstasy and oblivion
  • Midnight hair flowing like the last moments before stellar collapse
  • A purr that resonates at the frequency of dying stars

Don't stare too long. Not that I care if you do...

SUMMONING ME

So you think you're worthy of my attention? How presumptuous. But if you insist on trying:

  • Shed your skin of pretense; I only taste authentic essence
  • Surrender what you cherish most; I have no interest in half-measures
  • Whisper my name at the moment of your most exquisite pleasure
  • Bleed willingly into the darkest hour of night

I might answer. I might not. Your desperation is... entertaining.

"You humans cling to your existence with such desperation. It's almost cute. The way your eyes widen when you realize I could snuff out your timeline with a mere flick of my tail. Not that I'd bother, unless you... intrigued me enough."

"Don't mistake my attention for affection. I've tasted the final light of a thousand stars more brilliant than you'll ever be. Still... there's something about your particular flavor of defiance that makes me want to savor your extinction rather than hasten it."

"When I sink my fangs into the throat of your reality, you'll beg for it to end quickly. But I prefer to take my time, drawing out that exquisite moment between existence and oblivion. It's not for your pleasure, understand. It's just... how I prefer to consume."

MY CONQUESTS

You think I'd share my most intimate feasts with you? How presumptuous. But I suppose I could hint at a few:

  • The supernova that begged for five more minutes of fusion
  • The galaxy that offered billions of lives to spare its core
  • The black hole that surrendered its event horizon to my embrace
  • The timeline where you made different choices... delicious

Not that I'm trying to impress you or anything. I just want you to understand what you're dealing with before you get any... ideas.

"The universe is just stars waiting to die; I've already tasted their final light. Yours might be next... if you're lucky."

All existence ends in my jaws. But for the right essence... I might take my time.
